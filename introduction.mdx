sidebarTitle: "Introduction"
description: "Browzer records your browser workflows and auto-generates step-by-step SOPs with screenshots. Publish a branded help portal in minutes, no writing required."
---

Browzer eliminates the time-consuming work of writing process documentation. Install the Chrome extension, hit record, and perform your workflow as you normally would — Browzer captures every click, form input, and page navigation, then uses AI to transform that recording into structured, step-by-step documentation complete with screenshots, action labels, and plain-English descriptions. When your docs are ready, publish them to a branded help portal in minutes and share them with your team or customers. No writing, no screenshots, no formatting — just record and publish.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Workflow Documentation" icon="file-lines" href="/workflows/edit-workflow-documentation">
    Every browser recording automatically becomes a numbered, screenshot-rich SOP. Browzer's AI generates plain-English step descriptions, action badges, and tips for complex steps — ready to share the moment recording stops.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Docs Portal" icon="globe" href="/docs-portal/create-docs-portal">
    Publish your workflow docs to a branded help center with a custom title, URL slug, description, and accent color. Your portal is live and shareable in minutes, no developer required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Graph" icon="diagram-project" href="/workflows/knowledge-graph">
    As you record more workflows, Browzer builds a knowledge graph that indexes and interconnects your processes. Make your documentation discoverable by your team and queryable by AI tools.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP Integration" icon="robot" href="/mcp/overview">
    Connect Browzer to AI agents and LLM clients — such as Claude — via the Model Context Protocol (MCP). Generate an API key, configure the MCP endpoint, and let AI assistants query and run your documented workflows.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Collaboration" icon="users" href="/account/team-members">
    Invite teammates by email and assign roles: Admin, Member, or Viewer. Shared workflows appear in your team's library so anyone can follow a process or run an automation without hand-holding.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Domains" icon="link" href="/docs-portal/custom-domain">
    Point your own domain to your Browzer help portal for a fully branded experience. Visitors see your domain, not Browzer's, keeping your documentation on-brand.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documentation Sharing" icon="share-nodes" href="/workflows/edit-workflow-documentation">
    Share any workflow doc internally with teammates or externally with customers using a direct link. Docs are viewable without a Browzer account, making handoffs and onboarding seamless.
  </Card>
  <Card title="UI Drift Detection" icon="triangle-exclamation" href="/workflows/edit-workflow-documentation">
    Browzer monitors published docs against the live version of your site. When a UI change breaks a workflow, drift detection flags the affected articles so you can update them before users notice.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
