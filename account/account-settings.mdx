Your account settings in Browzer let you control how your profile appears across the workspace. The most important field here is your **display name** — this is the name that every other member of your organization sees next to your workflows, comments, and activity, so keeping it accurate and professional matters. Access your settings at any time through the **Account** link in the top navigation or sidebar.

## Update Your Display Name

Your display name is visible to every member of your organization the moment you save it. Follow the steps below to update it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Account Settings">
    Click **Account** in the top navigation bar or in the left sidebar. The account settings page opens, showing your current profile details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Display Name field">
    Click inside the **Display Name** input field. To replace the existing name entirely, press **Ctrl+A** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+A** (Mac) to select all text before you start typing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type your new display name">
    Enter the display name you want to use going forward. Choose something recognizable to your teammates — for example, your full name or preferred professional name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save Changes**. A confirmation message appears on the page to let you know the update was successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Your display name is visible to all organization members — it appears alongside your activity throughout Browzer. Use a professional name that your colleagues will recognize.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  After saving, the change takes effect immediately. You don't need to log out and back in — just look for the confirmation message on the page to verify the update went through.
</Tip>

## Verifying the Change

Once you click **Save Changes**, a confirmation message appears on the account settings page. If the page updates without showing an error banner, your new display name is live. You can navigate away and return to confirm the field shows your updated name. If other pages in Browzer still show your old name, do a hard browser refresh (**Ctrl+Shift+R** / **Cmd+Shift+R**) to clear any cached values.

## Common Issues

<Accordion title="The Account link isn't responding">
  Make sure you are fully logged in to Browzer. If the **Account** link appears unclickable or loads a blank page, try refreshing your browser. If the problem persists after a refresh, log out and log back in, then navigate to **Account** again.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The Display Name field is empty or won't accept input">
  Click directly inside the field to focus it. If it still won't accept input, try a different browser or disable any browser extensions that might be blocking form interactions. On some systems, a password-manager extension can interfere with editable text fields.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The Save Changes button didn't work">
  Ensure you've typed at least one character in the Display Name field — the button may be inactive if the field is blank. If the button appears active but nothing happens when you click it, check your browser console for errors or try a different browser. Contact Browzer support if the issue continues.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My old name is still showing in other parts of the app">
  After a successful save, most views update immediately. If you still see the old name elsewhere in Browzer, perform a hard browser refresh (**Ctrl+Shift+R** on Windows/Linux, **Cmd+Shift+R** on Mac) to force your browser to reload the latest data. Persistent display of an old name after a refresh is worth reporting to Browzer support.
</Accordion>
