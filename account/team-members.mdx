Managing your team in Browzer gives every colleague shared access to your workspace's recorded workflows, auto-generated SOPs, automations, docs portals, and schedules — all in one place. Only users with a **Team Owner** or **Admin** role can invite or remove members, so make sure you have the right permissions before you begin.

## Invite a Team Member

Send an email invitation to anyone you want to join your Browzer workspace. They'll receive a join link and appear in your team list straight away with a **Pending** status while they complete sign-up.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Team section">
    Click **Team** in the left sidebar navigation. The full list of current workspace members loads on this page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Select the **Invite Member** button near the top-right of the Team page to open the invitation input.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the email address">
    Type the full email address of the person you want to invite into the input field that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the entry">
    Click anywhere outside the email field to confirm the address. Browzer validates the format before letting you proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invitation**. The invited member is immediately added to your team list with a status of **Pending**, and they receive an email containing their unique join link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Invitations are sent one at a time. To add multiple colleagues, repeat the steps above for each person.
</Note>

## Remove a Team Member

When a colleague no longer needs access to your Browzer workspace, you can remove them from the team. Their access is revoked the moment you confirm the removal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Team section">
    Click **Team** in the left sidebar navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the member">
    Scroll through the member list to locate the person you want to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the member">
    Click the remove or delete icon next to their name and confirm the action when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Removing a member revokes their access to the workspace immediately. They will no longer be able to view or edit any workflows, docs portals, or other content in your Browzer account. This action cannot be undone — you must send a new invitation if you want to restore their access later.
</Warning>

## Common Issues

<Accordion title="The Invite Member button is grayed out">
  The **Invite Member** button is only active for users with a **Team Owner** or **Admin** role. If the button appears grayed out or disabled, your account has a lower permission level. Ask a Team Owner or Admin to either send the invitation on your behalf or upgrade your role first.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The invited member didn't receive their invitation email">
  Ask the invitee to check their spam or junk folder, as automated invitation emails sometimes get filtered. If the email isn't there, return to the **Team** page, find the member's **Pending** entry, and resend the invitation. Confirm you entered the correct email address before resending.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I invite multiple members at the same time?">
  Not currently — Browzer invitations are sent one at a time. Enter and send each address individually. There is no bulk-import or CSV upload option at this stage.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I clicked Send Invitation but nothing happened">
  First check that the email address was confirmed correctly by clicking outside the field before pressing **Send Invitation**. If the button still doesn't respond, try refreshing the page and repeating the steps. If the issue persists, contact Browzer support.
</Accordion>
