Browzer handles all payment processing through **Stripe**, a leading secure billing platform. Your subscription plan, payment method, and invoices are all managed directly in the Stripe billing portal — Browzer itself never stores your payment card details. You can reach the billing portal at any time via the **Billing** link in the left sidebar navigation.

## Access the Stripe Billing Portal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Billing section">
    Click **Billing** in the left sidebar navigation. The Billing page loads showing your current plan and the option to manage it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Billing">
    Select the **Manage Billing** button. Browzer securely redirects you to the Stripe billing portal in a new tab or the current window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in the Stripe portal">
    Once redirected, you have full control over your subscription, payment method, and invoices directly within the secure Stripe environment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrade or Downgrade Your Plan

Once you're in the Stripe billing portal, changing your subscription tier takes just a few clicks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Update subscription">
    Locate and click the **Update subscription** option within the Stripe portal. This opens the plan selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your new plan">
    Browse the available Browzer plans and click the one you want to switch to — whether that's upgrading to unlock more features or downgrading to reduce your spend.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Review the pricing summary and click the confirmation button to apply the change. Stripe processes the update immediately and displays a confirmation screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Plan changes typically take effect immediately. If your new plan isn't reflected inside Browzer right away, wait a minute and then refresh the Browzer app. See the troubleshooting section below if the issue persists.
</Note>

## What You Can Manage in the Stripe Portal

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Subscription Plan" icon="arrows-up-down">
    Upgrade to a higher tier for more workflows and features, or downgrade if your needs change. All plan options are listed in the Stripe portal under **Update subscription**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payment Method" icon="credit-card">
    Add or replace the credit or debit card on file. Stripe stores your card details securely — Browzer never has access to your raw card numbers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Invoices" icon="file-invoice">
    Download PDF invoices for any past billing period directly from the Stripe portal. Useful for expense reporting or accounting records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cancel Subscription" icon="circle-xmark">
    Cancel your Browzer subscription from within the Stripe portal. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  All billing is handled securely by Stripe. Browzer never stores or has access to your payment card details. If you have billing disputes or need to update tax information, contact Stripe support directly from within the portal.
</Info>

## Common Issues

<Accordion title="The Manage Billing button isn't responding">
  Make sure you're logged in to Browzer and that your browser isn't blocking pop-ups or redirects. The **Manage Billing** button opens the Stripe portal, which may be treated as a redirect by some browsers. If nothing happens when you click it, disable pop-up blockers for the Browzer domain and try again. If the button is still unresponsive, try a different browser or contact Browzer support.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My subscription change isn't reflected inside Browzer">
  After confirming a plan change in Stripe, return to Browzer and do a hard browser refresh (**Ctrl+Shift+R** on Windows/Linux, **Cmd+Shift+R** on Mac). Subscription updates are usually instantaneous, but can occasionally take a minute or two to propagate. If your plan still appears unchanged after five minutes, log out of Browzer and log back in. Contact Browzer support if the issue continues beyond that.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I didn't receive an invoice email">
  Stripe sends invoice emails to the address associated with your billing account. Check your spam or junk folder first. You can also download any past invoice directly from the Stripe portal — click **Billing** in Browzer, then **Manage Billing**, and locate the invoice history section in Stripe. If you need the invoice sent to a different email address, update your billing email within the Stripe portal.
</Accordion>
